MDF Keychain

MDF, or Medium-Density Fiberboard, is a type of engineered wood product made from wood fibers, wax, and resin. MDF keychains are small keychain-sized items made from this material. Here's some information about MDF keychains:
Material: MDF keychains are typically made from medium-density fiberboard, which is known for its durability and smooth surface.
Customization: MDF keychains can be customized with various designs, logos, or text using techniques like printing, engraving, or painting.
Cost-effective: MDF is an affordable material, making MDF keychains a budget-friendly option for promotional and branding purposes.

Hot Press Machine

A hot press machine, also known as a heat press machine, is a device used to transfer graphics, images, or designs onto various surfaces using heat and pressure. Here's some key information about hot press machines:
Operation: Hot press machines work by applying controlled heat and pressure to a transfer paper, which contains a design or graphic. This process transfers the design onto a substrate, such as fabric, ceramic, metal, or paper.
Applications: Hot press machines are widely used in various industries, including apparel printing, custom merchandise creation, sublimation printing, and heat transfer vinyl (HTV) applications. They are also used for heat sealing and laminating tasks.
Substrates: Hot press machines can be used on various substrates, including clothing, caps, mugs, ceramic tiles, mousepads, and more. The choice of substrate depends on the desired end product.

ID Card sublimation

ID card sublimation refers to the process of creating identification cards, such as employee badges, student IDs, or membership cards, using sublimation printing technology. Sublimation is a method of transferring ink onto the surface of a card, typically made of PVC or plastic, by converting it from a solid to a gas without passing through a liquid phase. Here's some information about ID card sublimation:
Card Material: ID cards for sublimation are usually made from PVC or plastic, as these materials can withstand the heat and pressure of the sublimation process. They come in various sizes and thicknesses.
Durability: Sublimation-printed ID cards are known for their durability and resistance to fading, scratching, and wear and tear. This makes them suitable for long-term use.

Sublimation Keychain

Printer

We are also dealings in Printers. Printers are devices used to produce physical copies of digital documents or images on paper. There are several types of printers, including:
Inkjet Printers: These use liquid ink to create high-quality color or black-and-white prints. They are suitable for both text and images.
Laser Printers: Laser printers use toner and heat to produce sharp, fast, and high-quality prints. They are often used in office environments for high-volume printing.
All-in-One Printers: These devices combine printing, scanning, copying, and sometimes faxing capabilities into a single machine, offering versatility for various tasks.
Thermal Printers: These printers use heat to produce images on special thermal paper, commonly seen in receipt printers and label printers.

Pen Sublimation

Pen sublimation is a printing process that allows for high-quality and full-color customization of pens. It involves transferring a design or image onto a special sublimation paper using sublimation ink. The paper is then wrapped around the pen, and heat and pressure are applied to transfer the ink from the paper onto the pen's surface. This process results in vibrant, long-lasting, and durable prints on pens, making it a popular choice for personalized promotional products and gifts.
